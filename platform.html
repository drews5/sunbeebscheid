Our Vision For Carlson

A student government that listens early, acts fast, and stays close to the people it serves. We are relentless about follow-through.

Real
Connections

Fostering real relationships and active listening over forced networking. Leadership shouldn't feel far away, and feedback shouldn't disappear.

  • Create more spaces for honest feedback, not just performative outreach.
  • Back student groups and cross-campus partnerships that build genuine friendships.
  • Keep the door open so students always know exactly where to bring an idea or concern.

Career
Success

Actionable plans to connect students with employers and resources in Minneapolis and beyond. Opportunities shouldn't stay scattered or feel transactional.

  • Push for tighter relationships with employers who explicitly want Carlson talent.
  • Make internships, treks, panels, and recruiting resources easier to find in a centralized hub.
  • Support rigorous programming that turns career prep into real access, not just passive advice.

Belonging
at Carlson

Ensuring every student feels at home and fully represented within the Carlson community. We want a school where more people can see themselves in campus life without pretending.

  • Champion a culture where diverse identities and backgrounds are recognized and elevated.
  • Support dedicated student leaders who are already doing the difficult, grassroots work of welcoming people in.
  • Advocate forcefully for communication, events, and traditions that feel distinctly human and inclusive.
