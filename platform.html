Our Vision For Carlson

Building a stronger community and student government that actually works for the people it serves.

REAL
CONNECTION

Real connections extend beyond networking. We want events that feel purposeful, policies that encourage connection, and constant feedback that actually leads to change.

  • Open up more ways to get honest feedback, and deliver it to school leaders.
  • Support student groups and campus partners that create community.
  • Keep the door open so students always know exactly what's going on at Carlson.

CAREER
SUCCESS

We're pushing for better career pathways starting the second you join Carlson. Professional development here should feel inclusive and tailored to your goals.

  • Push for more resources devoted to student internships and Carlson talent.
  • Bring internships, panels, and recruiting events into one easy central hub.
  • Focus on programming that opens doors and leads to long careers.

BELONGING

Every student should have the resources to feel at home here. We're building a culture where more people can see themselves in Carlson as authentically as possible.

  • Make sure different identities and backgrounds are recognized and supported.
  • Back the groups and leaders already welcoming people.
  • Advocate for communication and transparency that is human and inclusive.
