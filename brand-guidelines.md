# Brand Guidelines: Sunbeeb & Scheid

## Color Palette
- **Sky Blue (`#84b8d7`)**: Primary brand color. Use for backgrounds and large graphical elements.
- **Golden Yellow (`#ffcd33`)**: Primary accent. Use for highlighted words, calls to action, and distinct form boundaries.
- **Crimson Red (`#a22136`)**: High-contrast identifier. Used to spotlight countdowns, specific headings, and urgent notifications.

## Typography
- **Primary / Display**: Neutraface Text (Light, Book, Demi, Bold, plus Italics). Use for headings, logos, and critical callouts.
- **Secondary / Body**: Neutraface Text Book. Used entirely across the site.

## Logos
- Variations: `logo-light.png` (transparent/white text), `logo-dark.png` (transparent/dark text), `logo-square.png`.
- New: `logo-gold.png` for strict use on deeply colored primary gold backgrounds (`--gold`).

## Core Components
- Use `16px` border radii on all structural containers.
- Form inputs must leverage clear progressive disclosure. (One input displayed at a time).
- Staggered scroll reveals should trigger text blocks and imagery with subtle opacity and Y-transforms.

## Carlson Election Compliance Requirements
This digital platform and all campaign materials adhere strictly to the **Carlson School Election Rules**:
- **Trademarks (1.04.b):** The University of Minnesota block "M" or Goldy Gopher logo are strictly prohibited visually.
- **Data Privacy (1.06.a):** All collected voter information must be entirely secure, explicitly opt-in, properly vetted, and strictly never used for unsolicited automated mass messaging. 
- **Voting Coercion/Zones (1.04.e & 1.05.a):** Digital materials must gently encourage civic duty rather than coerce. The site must proactively warn students that no physical campaigning is allowed within 25 feet of designated voting-zones.
