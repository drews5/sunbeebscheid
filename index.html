We envision a Carlson that uplifts all students.

As President and Vice President of the Carlson undergraduate student body, we envision a Carlson that uplifts all students. This campaign is for everyone who believes in the strength of community and a student government that actually works for the people it serves.

We are advocating for real connections, career success, and a true sense of belonging in each corner of this school. We've seen what it looks like when students are overlooked, and we know what becomes possible when they are not. As your Presidential Team, we will work hard every single day to make that happen for all of you.

By representing Carlson students, we are here to listen carefully, act decisively, and deliver.
